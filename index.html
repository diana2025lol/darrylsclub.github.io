Q&A's

How to become staff?
Be active and engage with members. Consistent activity and helpfulness proves trustworthiness.
What happens if I break a rule?
Depending on severity, this can range from a warning to a ban. Severe actions like doxxing result in immediate ban.
Can I appeal a ban?
Yes, contact Mr.Black (@mr.black78) or any staff member you are friends with.
How do I use the bots?
Check the bot commands in your server channels. Each bot has its own prefix and help command.
How do I report a problem?
Contact staff in the support channel or DM a staff member directly.
