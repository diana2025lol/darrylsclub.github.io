❓ Q&A

How to become staff?

Engage positively, follow all rules, and be active in the server. Contact Higher Staff when ready to apply.

What happens if I break a rule?

Rule violations result in warnings, timeouts, or bans, depending on severity.

Can I appeal a ban?

Contact Mr.Black or any Higher Staff member you’re connected with. Appeals are reviewed fairly.

How do I verify my account?

Use the Verification section to submit your information to staff for approval.
