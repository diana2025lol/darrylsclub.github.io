Q&A's

How to become staff?
If you wish to become a staff member in our community, you must be active and engage with other members, to prove that you are worthy of a staff role..
What happens if I break a rule?
That can go from a warn to a ban, depending on the severity.
Can I appeal a ban?
It depends! Contact Mr.Black (@mr.black78) or any other staff you have friended.
